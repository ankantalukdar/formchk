What is FORMCHK?

FORMCHK is an AI-powered basketball shot analysis app designed to help players track, improve, and perfect their shooting technique. Simply upload a video of your shot, and the app provides detailed feedback and actionable tips.

FORMCHK uses advanced AI to analyze your shooting form from a video you upload. It identifies key areas for improvement and provides personalized feedback, drills, and progress tracking.
